Refund & Cancellation Policy

Transparent policies designed to protect both our users and our platform.

01 — Refund Policy

Refund Policy

  • Locklet Pro provides digital subscription services with features activated immediately upon successful payment.
  • Refund requests are reviewed in accordance with applicable laws, payment provider policies, and exceptional circumstances.
  • Because premium features are activated instantly after payment, refunds are generally not guaranteed once service has been activated.
  • Users experiencing technical issues are strongly encouraged to contact support before initiating a refund request.
  • Approved refunds will be processed via the original payment method used at the time of purchase.
  • Processing times may vary depending on the payment provider and your banking institution — typically 5–10 business days.

02 — Cancellation

Cancellation Policy

  • Users may cancel future subscription renewals at any time from account settings — no questions asked.
  • Cancellation does not result in immediate removal of access or deletion of data.
  • Your premium access remains fully active until the end of the current billing cycle.
  • After expiry, your account automatically reverts to the free plan with no action required.
  • All documents, files, and account data remain completely safe and accessible — we never delete your data without your explicit request.
