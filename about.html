Our Mission
Why Locklet Pro Exists

In a world where digital identity is everything, millions of Indians carry photos of their Aadhaar, PAN, and Driving Licence in unsecured gallery folders. Locklet Pro changes that — providing a beautiful, encrypted, AI-powered vault that makes document security effortless.

We believe every person deserves privacy and security for their personal documents — regardless of technical knowledge. That's why Locklet Pro is designed to be simple, beautiful, and powerful.
