Core Security
🔐
AES-256 Encrypted VaultCore

Every document stored in Locklet Pro is encrypted using military-grade AES-256 encryption — the same standard used by governments and banks worldwide. Your data is unreadable without your biometric authentication.

Military-grade Zero breach risk End-to-end
👆
Multi-Layer BiometricNew

Three independent layers of biometric protection: unlock the app with fingerprint/face, lock individual documents separately, and require auth before any cloud sync. Every level uses your device's native biometric hardware.

App-level lock Per-document lock Cloud sync lock
📵
Screenshot PreventionNew

Locklet Pro uses Android's FLAG_SECURE to completely block screenshots and screen recordings inside the app. Even screen mirroring is disabled — your documents stay private at all times.

Point your camera at any document — Aadhaar, PAN, Driving Licence, Voter ID — and Lucky's AI OCR engine instantly reads all text, numbers, and fields. No manual typing ever needed.

Cloud & Access
☁️
Secure Cloud Sync

Your documents are encrypted and synced to the cloud via Supabase. Access them on any device with your account. Every sync requires biometric authentication for maximum security.

📂
Offline Access

Don't have internet? No problem. All your documents are stored locally with full encryption. Access everything offline — your vault works anytime, anywhere, with zero connectivity needed.

🪪
All Document Types

Store any type of Indian identity document — Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Driving Licence, Voter ID, Passport, Marksheets, Certificates, and more. Locklet Pro supports them all.
